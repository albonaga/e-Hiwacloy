e-Hiwacloy Team Reflection

This section presents our experiences, challenges, learnings, and growth throughout the development of the
e-Hiwacloy system, from system prototype creation up to creating our final project web reporting site.

Overall Reflection

Throughout the development of the e-Hiwacloy system, our team gained valuable experience in usability testing, interface design, teamwork, data analysis, and web development.

The project helped us understand the importance of creating systems that are not only functional, but also user-friendly, accessible, and responsive to community healthcare needs.

What We Learned

  • Importance of usability evaluation
  • How user feedback improves systems
  • Creating responsive web interfaces
  • Applying SUS evaluation methods
  • Improving teamwork and communication

Challenges We Faced

  • Fixing system and layout issues
  • Managing group schedules
  • Analyzing SUS responses accurately
  • Improving responsiveness of pages
  • Organizing documentation and findings

Development Experience

  • Created the prototype interface
  • Designed charts and visual reports
  • Integrated data gathering sections
  • Improved navigation and usability
  • Enhanced the final reporting website

Skills We Improved

  • HTML, CSS, and JavaScript skills
  • UI/UX design principles
  • Problem-solving and debugging
  • Research and documentation
  • Presentation and collaboration
